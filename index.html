Top Attractions in Cairo

  • City: Cairo
  • Suggested Visit Time: 2-3 days
  • Cost: Moderate
  • Best Season: All year

Cairo, the capital of Egypt, is a city full of history, culture, and amazing sights. Visitors can explore ancient monuments, vibrant markets, and historic neighborhoods.

From the iconic Pyramids of Giza to the bustling streets of Khan El Khalili, Cairo offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler. Planning ahead will help you enjoy the city to its fullest.

Pyramids of Giza

The Pyramids of Giza are ancient wonders located in Giza. They are a must-see when visiting Cairo.

  • Location: Giza
  • Best time: Morning
  • Entrance Fee: ~200 EGP

Egyptian Museum

The Egyptian Museum houses the world's largest collection of pharaonic antiquities.

  • Location: Tahrir Square
  • Best time: Afternoon
  • Entrance Fee: ~160 EGP

Khan El Khalili Bazaar

A famous market in Cairo where you can shop for souvenirs, spices, and traditional crafts.

  • Location: Islamic Cairo
  • Best time: Evening
  • Cost: Free to enter, shopping extra

Cairo Citadel

The Citadel of Cairo is a historic fortress with mosques and museums, offering panoramic views of the city.

  • Location: Islamic Cairo
  • Best time: Afternoon
  • Entrance Fee: ~180 EGP

Tips for Visitors

It's best to start your day early to avoid the crowds, wear comfortable shoes for walking, and stay hydrated. For shopping at Khan El Khalili, be ready to haggle for better prices.

Exploring Cairo's top attractions will give you a deep appreciation of the city's rich history and vibrant culture, making it a must-visit destination for every traveler.
